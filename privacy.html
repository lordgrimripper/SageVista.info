Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SageVista ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide on our website. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, apply for a job, or contact us. The personal information we collect may include:

  • - Name, email address, and phone number
  • - City, state, and age
  • - Highest qualification and work experience
  • - Preferred job type and current job status
  • - Your resume or CV and any other files you upload
  • - Information about how you heard about us

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • - Match your profile with suitable job opportunities.
  • - Provide personalized career guidance and support.
  • - Communicate with you regarding job applications and services.
  • - Connect employers with potential candidates.
  • - Improve our website and services based on user feedback.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a third-party service, Formsubmit.co, to handle form submissions securely. The information you provide is sent directly to our designated email address and is not publicly stored or shared on the website.

Sharing of Information

We will share your information, including your resume, with prospective employers only with your consent and for the purpose of job applications. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to do so, please contact us at sagevistaconsulting@gmail.com.
