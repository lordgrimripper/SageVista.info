Frequently Asked Questions

What is SageVista?

SageVista is a professional job consultancy and career guidance firm dedicated to providing the "Right Path" for your career journey. We offer personalized recruitment solutions and expert career advice to help you find the perfect fit.

What services do you provide?

Our core services include comprehensive job placement for candidates, efficient recruitment solutions for businesses, and personalized career guidance, including resume building, interview coaching, and long-term career planning.

How can I apply for a job?

You can easily apply by filling out the Job Seeker form in our "Contact" section. Please be sure to upload your resume so our dedicated team can review your qualifications and match you with the best opportunities.

Do you only help freshers?

No, our services are designed to help professionals at all stages of their careers. We successfully place both freshers seeking their first job and experienced professionals looking for a new challenge or career advancement.

Do you charge any fees?

We charge a minimal fee to candidates only after they have been successfully placed in a role. This allows us to maintain a high-quality, ethical, and dedicated service for all our clients.

Which industries do you cover?

We cover a wide array of industries, including IT, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Logistics, Education, BPO/Call Centers, Retail, and Hospitality. Our expansive network is constantly growing to provide you with more career options.

What is the typical job placement timeline?

The timeline for placement varies depending on your specific profile and the requirements of the role. However, our team works diligently to ensure the process is as efficient and fast as possible, keeping you informed at every step.
